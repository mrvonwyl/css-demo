sticky
  • sticks within the space of its parent
  • at least one helper property must be defined
  • kept in document flow

static

  • default (nothing special)

relative

relative
  • relative to its original position
  • kept in document flow

absolute

absolute
  • relative to its closest non-static parent
  • removed from document flow

fixed

fixed
  • relative to viewport
  • removed from document flow
